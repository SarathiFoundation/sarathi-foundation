WHO WE ARE?
We are 'SARATHI FOUNDATION' a Non-Profit Charity organizations tion, started by young people like Paris badave and his young warrior friends. We belive that "Giving is not just about making donation, its about making a difference." We are driven to help poor,needy,disabled people ,we enable individuals and organizations to raise and donate funds.
We have helped hundreds of poor people during Covid-19 by providing safety kits,clothes,food,etc.

OUR MISSION?
Our mission is to reach out helpless and needy people and help them every possible way, we are motivating and empowering people to make difference in society.

FUTURE PLANS?
We're planning several campaigns like blood donation camp ,daily village check up camp, reach out people affected by ﬂood and natural calamities. And run donation campaigns on bigger level.
